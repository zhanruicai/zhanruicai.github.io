I am a tenure-track assistant professor in the [Faculty of Business and Economics](https://www.hkubs.hku.hk/glocal/) at the The University of Hong Kong. My home department is Innovation and Information Management [Business Analytics](https://www.hkubs.hku.hk/people/zhanrui-cai/). I am also part of the [Statistics group](https://hkubs-stat.github.io/).

## Professional Experience

- **Assistant Professor**, The University of Hong Kong, 2023 - present.

## Research Interests

My research broadly lies at the interface of statistics and machine learning. I am generally interested in statistics for AI, especially large language models, statistical inference under differential privacy, and leveraging machine learning methods to improve the efficiency of statistical procedures.
